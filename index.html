Geolocated &
Probabilistic Weather

Each CardanoTree is located at a coordinate on the world map (based on latitude and longitude).
That position on the map determines:
- day / night cycles
- flower and fruit season
- probabilistic weather conditions based on historical data

Evolutive Art

Are not static pics, gifs or videos... it is evolutive art because they grow over time!
Grow, bear flowers 🌻 or fruit🥝, shrink at some season and grow back!
They also experience different weather conditions like real trees:
- rain ⛈️ ☔
- snow 🌨️ ❄️ ☃️
- during the day 🌞 and night🌛
Using generative art techniques, the whole tree that evolves and lives forever are generated with only 4kb code (html+js).

Full On-Chain

Using generative art techniques, the whole tree that evolves and lives forever are generated with only 4kb code.
Due to the small size code, it is possible to mint it fully onchain! No IPFS required!
As long as the Cardano blockchain exists, this tree will live on it, and it will continue to grow and change forever.
